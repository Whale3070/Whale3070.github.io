雅思写作课程-第十二课-大作文的第一段

首先，找出key word，关键词语和关键词中的联系
其次，转述题目的内容
最后陈述你的观点并在文章中解释你的观点。

题目

Environmental problems, such as pollution and climate change, are global issues that require collective efforts to solve. However, some people argue that individuals have a greater responsibility than governments and international organizations in addressing these problems. To what extent do you agree or disagree with this statement? Provide relevant examples and support your opinion.
环境问题，如污染和气候变化，是需要集体努力解决的全球性问题。然而，一些人认为，在解决这些问题方面，个人比政府和国际组织负有更大的责任。你在多大程度上同意或不同意这一说法？提供相关的例子并支持您的意见。

第一段
In this society, Earth has many environmental problems, and there are many people who believe we should do more to protect the Earth. Furthermore, they think the governments can only do little, but everyone needs to do more to change their behavior regarding nature protection. I agree with this point of view. This essay will delve into the contrasting perspectives regarding the impact of every individual on environmental protection. By examining both viewpoints, I will present my own opinion on this significant issue.
在这个社会中，地球面临许多环境问题，有很多人认为我们应该做更多来保护地球。此外，他们认为政府只能做很少的事情，而每个人都需要做更多来改变他们对自然保护的行为。我同意这个观点。本文将深入探讨关于每个人对环境保护的影响的对立观点。通过分析两种观点，我将提出自己在这个重要问题上的观点。

题目二

some people think that only electric cars should be allowed on the road by 2040.

第一段
In recent years, electric cars have become more and more popular in this society. Although electric car technology has improved a lot, I do not agree that petroleum cars should be forbidden on the road by 2040. This essay will delve into the contrasting perspectives regarding the impact of which fuel cars should be allowed on the road. By examining both viewpoints, I will present my own opinion on this significant issue.

题目3

some people think there should be free health care for all people, but others disagree. disscuss both sides.

第一段
It’s a good idea that everyone should have access to free healthcare, especially for poor people. However, doctors need to receive a salary as a reward for their diligent work. I do not agree that doctors and nurses should work for free. As a result, citizens should contribute financially to hospitals to support the medical system. This essay will delve into the contrasting perspectives regarding the impact of non healthcare expenses for all people. By examining both viewpoints, I will present my own opinion on this significant issue.
每个人都应该获得免费医疗保健，这是一个好主意，尤其是对穷人来说。然而，医生需要获得工资作为对他们勤奋工作的奖励。我不同意医生和护士应该免费工作。因此，公民应该向医院捐款，以支持医疗系统。本文将深入探讨医疗费用对所有人影响的对比视角。通过考察这两种观点，我将对这一重大问题发表我自己的看法。
