  1. Her hands are very small.
    错误选项：is very

    她的手很小。

  2. My brother doesn’t work in an office
    错误选项：isn’t work

    我的兄弟没有在办公室工作。

  3. Susana thinks that vegetables with rice are healthier than vegetables with pasta.
    错误选项：rices
    rice是不可数名字，没有复数形式

    Susana认为蔬菜+米饭比蔬菜+披萨要健康

  4. The Buchwalds used to live in this neighborhood. They moved away 6 months ago.
    错误选项：were use to

    Buchwalds曾经居住在这附近，他们六个月前搬走了。

  5. Can you open the door, please? It must be lzzy. She said she would come by.
    错误选项：can’t

    你能打开门吗？肯定是lzzy来了，她说了她将会来。

  6. The team will join the race, although they struggled to make time for training.
    错误选项：even

    这个队伍将会参加比赛，虽然他们连训练的时间都没有。

  7. She thought he was going to give her a ring for Christmas.
    错误选项：is going

    她认为，他将会在圣诞节给她一个戒指。

    thought是think的过去式，是曾经想过，因此用was going to，过去进行时。

  8. A year from now, they will be chasing after their industry’s main award in an attempt to repeat last year’s success.
    错误选项：are chasing

    一年后，他们将会追逐行业的主要奖项，试图重复去年的成功。

  9. My advice is to file a complaint at once. That behavior is unacceptable.
    错误选项：is filing

    我的建议是立即提出投诉。这种行为是不可接受的。

  10. It’s time they took note of past mistakes and got their act together quickly.
    错误选项：It’s the time

    现在是他们记下过去的错误并迅速采取行动的时候了。

    It’s the time to do sth是固定用法，句子里没有to，因此选择It’s time

  11. The money disappeared, which puzzled the CFO. The CPO was equally puzzled by the disappearance of their funds
    错误选项：puzzling about their disappeared funds

    钱不见了，这让首席财务官感到困惑。CPO同样对他们的资金消失感到困惑

    equally，同样的。这个词说明前后的时态应该保持一致。前面的句子用puzzled，后面的句子也用puzzled而不是puzzling。

  12. They would not have purchased this land if they had been told about the lack of water here.
    错误选项：have been told

    如果他们被告知这里缺水，他们就不会购买这片土地。

  13. Should they decide to take an earlier flight, they would need to call us immediately.
    错误选项：In case decide

    如果他们决定乘坐更早的航班，他们需要立即致电我们。

  14. Svenson looked as though he had slept a mere 4 hours.
    错误选项：as though if

    斯文森看起来好像只睡了4个小时。

    as though常表示一些已经确定的消息

    as if由它本身所带的“如果”的含义，表示了一种对假设信息的不确定。

  15. He would have sustained lifelong trauma but for the nurse’s prompt and sensitive action.
    错误选项：because of
    but for 要不是；若非

    如果不是护士的迅速和敏感的行动，他会遭受终生的创伤。

  16. Would you **be so kind as to ** ensure that we don’t lose momentum on this? Time is of the essence.
    错误选项：show kindness to

    你会好心地确保我们不会在这方面失去动力吗？时间至关重要。

  17. In essence, the results were inconclusive and, insofar as we can see now, more research will need to be conducted.
    错误选项：sofar as

    从本质上讲，结果尚无定论，就我们现在所看到的而言，需要进行更多的研究。

  18. In the long run, the absurdity of investing in wall-mounted heating will become self-evident.
    错误选项：In course of the time

    从长远来看，投资壁挂式供暖的荒谬性将变得不言而喻。
