<p>Check whether you have <code>Ruby 2.1.0</code> or higher installed:</p>
<div class="highlight highlight-source-shell"><pre>ruby --version</pre></div>
<p>Install <code>Bundler</code>:</p>
<div class="highlight highlight-source-shell"><pre>gem install bundler</pre></div>
<p>Clone Jacman theme:</p>
<div class="highlight highlight-source-shell"><pre>git clone https://github.com/Simpleyyt/jekyll-theme-next.git
<span class="pl-c1">cd</span> jekyll-theme-next</pre></div>
<p>Install Jekyll and other dependencies from the GitHub Pages gem:</p>
<div class="highlight highlight-source-shell"><pre>bundle install</pre></div>
<p>Run your Jekyll site locally:</p>
<div class="highlight highlight-source-shell"><pre>bundle <span class="pl-c1">exec</span> jekyll server</pre></div>

<p>Show a feed link.</p>

<p>Set <code>rss</code> field in theme's <code>_config.yml</code>, as the following value:</p>
